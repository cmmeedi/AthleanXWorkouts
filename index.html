Biceps

The Biceps consists of three different muscles

  1. The Brachialis
  2. The Longhead
  3. The Shorthead

The longhead has some attachments in the shoulder which can allow more of a deeper workout by positioning the elbow behind the body


When doing curls the Long Head can be better activated by curling inward. Think of French Fries and not Pizza


Similar is the Short Head which can be better activated by curling outward. Think of Pizza and not French Fries

The bicep is a Flexor which means it is flexed when the elbow bends.


it is also a supinator. There is a Tendon that goes from the forearm into the bicep and because there are fibers in the bicep that are attached to the shoulder, then by extending the shoulder further contracts the bicep.


Pronate(palm facing down) misaligns the fore arm and the bicep. Supinating (palm facing up) aligns it allowing more contraction of the bicep
